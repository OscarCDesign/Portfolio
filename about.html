BACKSTORY

You haven't seen any of my projects yet. I see you're very interested in learning about me, in that case... I was born in Guadalajara, Mexico (2000), I pursued a degree in Design—not merely as a craft-focused discipline, but as a strategic framework for architecting sustainable solutions. My approach is rooted in uncovering the latent needs buried within complex layers of research to build meaningful user experiences.

My background divides between Brand Strategy and Product Design. Experience at a specialized marketing agency sharpened my understanding of brand positioning teaching me to desing with an intention; There for, while you may browse hundreds of portfolios, this one will persist in your memory as the orange one or, ideally, as Oscar C. I pivoted toward scalable UI and rigorous UX Research. My performance during residency led to an immediate post-grad hire as a Design Partner. Since then, I have spearheaded the end-to-end design lifecycle for diverse projects—from initial stakeholder alignment and requirement gathering to high-fidelity prototyping and iterative usability testing.

In my free time, I'm a fan of great stories and creative ideas (it's just a way of saying I love watching series, movies, and playing video games). I enjoy being creative in different ways: drawing, painting, animating, writing, sculpting, and I really enjoy building LEGOs—though there's little creativity in following an instruction manual.
